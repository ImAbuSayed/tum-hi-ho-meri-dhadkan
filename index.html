Song
Tum Hi Ho Meri Dhadkan
Lyrics
Teri aankhon mein basa hai jahaan, woh jahaan mera hai
Dil ki dhadkan teri awaaz, har pal mujhko bulati hai
Chandni raaton mein tera saath, khwahishein yeh badhti hai
Ek duje ke bin ab yeh zameen, adhoori si lagti hai
Tum hi ho, meri dhadkan, haan tum hi ho
Har nazar mein bas tera chehra, ab toh yeh dil roke
Nachle jab tum, hawaaon mein, rang yeh jhoome
Tere bina lamhe, beetein kaise? Poochhe yeh dhadkan!
Raste milte hain jab tumse, manzilein khud chalti hain
Teri muskaan pe likha hai, mera kal aur aaj bhi
Baahon mein teri aasmaan, haatho se chhua hai chaand
Yeh mohabbat ki kahani, ab toh hai sabki zubaan
Kyun dooriyan bhi paas lagti hain?
Tera har gham bhi geet ban jaaye
Meri saanson ko tham le, ke ab tera hi sahara
Yeh zindagi teri raahon ka intezaar karegi…
Tum hi ho… meri dhadkan…
Har pal tum, har saans tum…
Jeevan ka yeh sangeet, ab tum hi ho…
Tum hi ho…
